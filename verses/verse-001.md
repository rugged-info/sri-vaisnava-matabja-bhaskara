# Verse 1

### Sanskrit (Devanāgarī)
धर्मस्य तत्त्वं निहितं गुहायाम्।

### IAST
dharmasya tattvaṃ nihitaṃ guhāyām।

### Word-by-word (with grammar)
- **dharmasya** — “of dharma” (gen. sg. masc.)
- **tattvam** — “essence, truth” (acc. sg. neut.)
- **nihitam** — “placed, hidden” (ppp, acc. sg. neut.)
- **guhāyām** — “in the cave/heart” (loc. sg. fem.)

### Literal translation
The essence of dharma is placed in the cave (of the heart).

### Smooth translation
The truth of dharma lies hidden within the heart.

### Notes
- Example note about how “guhā” is glossed in this tradition.
