### Line 1
Devanagari: प्रातिःस्मरणम  
IAST: prātiḥsmaraṇam  
English: Morning remembrance


### Line 3
Devanagari: प्रािः स्मरातम रघुनाथमुखारतवन्दों मन्दब्लस्मिों मिुरभातष ववशालभालम्।  
IAST: prāiḥ smarātama raghunāthamukhāratavandōṁ mandablasmiyoṁ murabhātaṣa vivśālābhālam।  
English: O remembrance, may I worship the glorious face of Raghunatha, surrounded by the gentle fragrance of the virtuous.

### Line 4
Devanagari: कणाचवलब्लिर्लकु ण्डलशोतभगण्डों कणाचन्तदीघचनयनों नयनातभरामम्।। 1 ।।  
IAST: kaṇācavalablirlakunḍalaśotabhagaṇḍoṃ kaṇācantadīghacanayanonaṃ nayānātabharāmam।। 1 ।।  
English: With shining eyes resembling a lotus, long and delicate, glistening like a gem, may I be filled with the grace that flows from them.  


### Line 5
Devanagari: प्रािभचजातम रघुनाथकरारतवन्दों रिोगणाय भयदों वरदों तनजेभ्यः ।  
IAST: prābhiçajātaṃ raghunāthakarāratavandōṃ riōgaṇāya bhayadōṃ varadōṃ tanjēbhyah।  
English: To those who have taken refuge in the worship of Lord Raghunatha, who dispels fear and grants boons, I offer my respects.

### Line 6
Devanagari: यद्राजसोंसतद तवभज्य मिेशर्ापों सीिाकारग्रिणमङ्गलमाप सद्यः ।।2।।  
IAST: yadrājasonsa-tad tavabhajya miśerāpóṃ sīcāragrīṇamaṅgalamāpa sadyaḥ।।2।।  
English: O King, upon that which is offered to you, may the auspiciousness of the divine ones come immediately.

### Line 7
Devanagari: प्रािनचमातम रघुनाथपदारतवन्दों वज्राङ्कु शातदशुभरेब्लख सुखाविों मे।  
IAST: prāṇicamatama raghunāthapadāratavandoṁ vajrāṅkuśātadaśubhareblak sukhāviṁs me.  
English: O great essence of life, I bow at the feet of Lord Raghu-nātha; may the thunderbolt bring auspiciousness and happiness to me.

### Line 8
Devanagari: योगीन्द्रमानसमिवृत्तसेव्यमानों शापामुच्यमानः पतितो गोमयमिच्छपत्नीः ॥ 3 ॥  
IAST: yogīndramānasamivṛttasevyamānāḥ śāpāmucyamānaḥ patito gomayamiccha-patnīḥ ॥ 3 ॥  
English: The yogi, engaged in the service of the enlightened mind, is freed from curses, and falls like a clay figure seeking a wife.

### Line 9
Devanagari: प्रािवचदातम वर्सा रघुनाथनाम वाग्दोषिारर सकलों शमलों तनिब्लन्त ।  
IAST: prāvivcadātama varṣā raghunāthanāma vāgdoṣārār sakaloṃ śamaloṃ tanibhlanta   
English: The most auspicious of times, the year of the name of Raghunatha, when all virtues are propitious and all actions prosper.

### Line 10
Devanagari: यत्पावचती स्वपवतना सह मोक्षकामा प्रीत्या सिस्रिररनामसमों जजाप ।।4।।
IAST: yatpāvacatī svapavatnā saha mokṣakāmā prītyā sisriraranāmasamoṁ jajāpa
English: The one who is purified by the power of truth, desiring liberation, chanting the names with affection, has attained it.

### Line 11
Devanagari: प्रािः श्रये श्रुतिनुिाों रघुनाथमूतिच नीलाम्बुदोत्पलवसतेतररत्ननीलाम्।  
IAST: prā́iḥ śraye śrutinunā́ṁ rāghunāthamuticā nīlāmbudotpala-vasate-tara-ratna-nīlām.  
English: I am devoted to the deep blue of the lotus blooming in the ocean of the Lord Raghunatha, the essence of all scriptures.

### Line 12
Devanagari: आमुक्तमौब्लक्तकतवशेषतवभूषणाढ्ाों ध्येयाों समस्तमुतनतभजचनमुब्लक्तिेिुम् ।।5।।
IAST: āmuktamauḍblaktakatavaśeṣatvabhūṣaṇāḍhāṁ dhyeāṁ samastamutanatabhajacanamublaktiḥ ।
English: Let one contemplate the divine form adorned with special ornaments that are free from impediments and delusions, as it is worshipped by all forms of beings.

### Line 13
Devanagari: यः श्लोकपञ्चकतमदों प्रयिः पठेब्लद्ध तनत्यों प्रभातसमये पुरुष प्रबुद्धः ।  
IAST: yaḥ ślokapañcakatamadon pratiḥ paṭhebladdha tanatyon prabhātasamayē puruṣa prabuddhaḥ ।  
English: He who reads this quintet of verses in the early morning becomes enlightened like a man awakened.  

### Line 14
Devanagari: श्रीरामवकङ्करजनेषु स एव मुख्यो भूत्वा प्रयावत िररलोकमनन्यलभ्यम्।।6।।
IAST: śrīrāmavakaṅkara-janeṣu sa eva mukhyo bhūtvā prayāvati ralarokamananyalabhyam।।6।।
English: Indeed, among the followers of Lord Rama, he becomes the principal, striving to attain that which is uniquely achievable in the world.
